ELE6209A · Winter 2026

Systèmes de navigation

Graduate course on navigation systems, with emphasis on probabilistic state estimation, sensor fusion, inertial navigation, and practical architectures for modern navigation.

Course Topics

  • Probabilistic state estimation and uncertainty propagation
  • Inertial measurement units and navigation frames
  • Sensor fusion for positioning and attitude estimation
  • Kalman filtering and related recursive estimators
