Visitor statistics

This website uses MapMyVisitors and a private visitor analytics service hosted on Cloudflare. These services may process an IP address, approximate country and city, network organization, page path, referring page, browser information, language, and visit time.

The private analytics data is used only for internal audience statistics and website security, is not displayed publicly, does not use cookies, and is automatically deleted after 90 days. Visitors who enable Global Privacy Control or the browser Do Not Track setting are not recorded by the private analytics service.
