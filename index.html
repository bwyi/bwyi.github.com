About

My work focuses on the estimation, learning, and control of nonlinear dynamical systems, with applications in robotics and autonomous systems. I am affiliated with GERAD and the Mobile Robotics and Autonomous Systems Laboratory.

I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. Prior to joining Polytechnique Montréal, I held research positions at CNRS–CentraleSupélec (France), the Australian Centre for Robotics (University of Sydney), and the Robotics Institute at the University of Technology Sydney (Australia).

I was the recipient of the IEEE CCTA Best Student Paper Award (IEEE Control Systems Society, 2019) and the Discovery Early Career Researcher Award (DECRA) from the Australian Research Council in 2024.

Research Themes

Nonlinear Control Reliable feedback design for complex, uncertain dynamical systems. Robotics Estimation and control methods for autonomous and mobile robotic systems. Learning & Estimation Data-enabled methods with an emphasis on stability, structure, and guarantees.
