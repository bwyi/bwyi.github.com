Open Positions

I am always interested in working with students and researchers who are interested in control, estimation, learning, and robotics. My group values strong theoretical foundations, clarity of thinking, and a genuine interest in solving meaningful problems.

Prospective candidates are encouraged to read this page carefully before contacting me.

Ph.D. and M.Sc. Students

I am regularly looking for Ph.D. and M.Sc. students to join my group at Polytechnique Montréal. Successful candidates typically have a strong background in control systems, applied mathematics, robotics, or related fields, together with a genuine curiosity about dynamical systems and control.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral positions are occasionally available, subject to funding. Strong candidates are encouraged to contact me to discuss potential collaboration and funding opportunities. The best option is to consider competitive postdoctoral funding opportunities in Canada and Québec, include the Banting Postdoctoral Fellowship, NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowship, Mitacs Elevate, FRQNT Postdoctoral Scholarship, IVADO Postdoctoral Scholarship, and GERAD Fellowship.

Visiting Students and Research Interns

I welcome visiting graduate students, undergraduate interns, and academic visitors for short- or long-term research stays. Visiting students are typically expected to have external funding from their home institution or scholarship programs.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining my group, please send me a brief email with the subject line “Prospective Student / Researcher” and include the following information:

  • A short description of your research interests
  • Your CV
  • Academic transcripts (unofficial is sufficient)
  • Links to publications or preprints (if available)

Please note that formal admission to graduate programs at Polytechnique Montréal is handled centrally by the university, and meeting the program admission requirements is mandatory.
