ELE6214 · Winter 2025 Updated for Winter 2025

Commande de systèmes incertains

Language of instruction for Winter 2025: English.

Course Objectives

  • Understand uncertainties in control systems.
  • Learn the theory and practice of mainstream techniques for designing control systems with uncertainties:
    • Adaptive control
    • Online parameter estimation and learning
    • Robust control

Textbooks and Reference Materials
