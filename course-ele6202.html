ELE6202 · Fall 2024-2025

Systèmes multivariables

Graduate course on multivariable systems, state-space modeling, and analysis tools for modern control.

Course Topics

  • State-space representations of linear systems
  • Controllability, observability, and minimal realizations
  • Multivariable feedback and robustness concepts
  • Frequency-domain and time-domain analysis

  • Lecture 2: Mathematics Review
